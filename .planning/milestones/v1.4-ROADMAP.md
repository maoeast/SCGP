# Milestone v1.4: Dashboard Special Ed Command Center

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-03-19

## Overview

v1.4 reshapes the homepage into a real special-education daily command center. The milestone removes mock-style placeholders, pulls all homepage business signals from the local database, and lets teachers launch training directly from today's schedule into the concrete runtime.

## Phases

### Phase 11: Dashboard Special Ed Command Center

**Depends on**: `sys_training_plan`, `sys_plan_resource_map`, `training_records`, emotional session tables, assessment tables  
**Plans**: 1 plan

Plans:

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- The homepage must use real SQL-backed data only; empty states are preferable to invented placeholder rows.
- The current schema does not justify fake time-of-day schedule rendering; date-range schedule is the truthful MVP.
- Direct launch should be implemented once as a shared routing contract, not duplicated separately in Dashboard and Plan pages.

**Issues Resolved:**

- The old homepage no longer centers low-value ERP summaries such as recently added students.
- Teachers can now see schedule pressure, assessment debt, and intervention risk from one page.
- Today's schedule can launch concrete training pages directly without forcing a second student/module selection flow.

**Issues Deferred:**

- The dashboard still launches the first eligible plan resource rather than a richer priority/recommendation strategy.
- Repository-wide historical `vue-tsc` errors remain outside the v1.4 touched-file scope.
